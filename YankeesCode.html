March 12th, 2017

George M. Steinbrenner Field

Yankees vs. Braves

Preview:

Today, the first place Yankees square off against the last place Braves at the new Geoge M. Steinbrenner Field. The Yankee, sporting a 13-3 record, will play behind former ace C.C. Sabathia on the mound. The 36-year-old Sabathia is in the last year of his contract and hasn't had a winning season since 2013. He made 30 starts in 2016, going 9-12 with a 3.91 ERA. He is 223-141 overall in his 16-year career.

The Braves on the other hand have a record of 5-10-1. On the mound will be the former St. Louis Cardinals pitcher, Jaime Garcia. Last season, Jaime pitched to 10-13 W-L record with an ugly 4.67 ERA. However, in the 2015 campaign, Jamie had a 10-6 record with an impressive 2.43 ERA through around 130 innings.

Final Score: Braves 10 - Yankees 2
